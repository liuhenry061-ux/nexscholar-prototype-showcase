What changed from the current site

The live site already has research content, grants, events, and scholarships. This prototype keeps that ecosystem but reorganises it around decision support.

Before

Users could browse opportunities, but the path from discovery to a confident supervisor or candidate decision was unclear.

After

Users choose a goal early, then matching, comparison, and onboarding adapt around that goal.

Result

More clarity, faster shortlisting, and better return triggers through activity indicators and profile completion prompts.

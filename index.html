The four enhancement pillars

The project charter asked for better AI matching presentation, profile comparison, onboarding, and engagement. This home page acts as a stitched overview of those four prototype areas.

Explainable matching

Move from generic recommendation lists to score cards that explain research fit, method overlap, and readiness level.

Open matching page

Decision support comparison

Give students and lecturers a focused side-by-side view instead of making them jump between disconnected profiles.

Open comparison page

Guided onboarding

Replace text-heavy first-use instructions with a short, clickable journey based on the user's goal and role.

Open onboarding page

Useful engagement prompts

Make notifications feel academic and actionable: improve a profile, respond to a good match, or save a relevant opportunity.

Integrated across pages

What changed from the current website

The live NexScholar site is already a content-rich platform. The enhancement does not throw that away. It reorganises the experience around decision support.

Before

Users see opportunities and content, but it is less obvious how to move from discovery into supervisor or candidate decisions.

After

Users choose a goal early and the system shapes recommendations, onboarding, and next actions around that goal.

Result

More confidence for first-time users, more clarity for matching, and stronger reasons to return to the platform.
